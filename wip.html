Frictionless Super Memory

Draft

Never lose an idea again. A new definition of what it means to be organized, built on AI tools that track all the open loops, enforce deadlines, and surface what matters.

  • Tag things with hashtags in your text messages so they get remembered
  • Highlight key moments in conversations — have AI grab the good quotes
  • Track app names and links that are new
  • Track problems mentioned across conversations
  • Voice record passively while meditating, thoughtstream while driving
  • Queue @targets to aggregate meaningful stuff
  • Capture things immediately — don't try to find the right file
  • Set up automatic follow-ups on your friends
  • Have your bot tell people your status when you're busy, that you care about them
  • Track all things with deadlines and enforce them

It's not just a tool but a system. Tools are going to be commodities. Innovation and ideas are the advancements.
